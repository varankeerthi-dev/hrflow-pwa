## Design Context

### Users
- **HR Staff & Employees**: Managing fleet logistics and maintenance across both Desktop (auditing) and Mobile (field entry).

### Brand Personality
- **Sleek, Technical, Transparent**: The interface should feel like a high-performance logistics instrument.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Brutally Minimal**: Focus on extreme high-contrast typography, generous negative space, and a refined "ZINC + INDIGO" palette. 
- **Typography-First**: Using font weights and sizes rather than borders or heavy cards to create hierarchy.

### Design Principles
1. **Temporal Priority**: Due dates (Insurance & Service) are the primary visual anchors.
2. **Horizontal Efficiency**: Flattened hierarchies to keep the manifest readable at a glance without nesting.
3. **Tactile Feedback**: Interactive states should feel instant and sleek, using subtle OKLCH color transitions.
4. **Mobile Utility**: Data-dense tables that collapse into high-contrast text stacks for field use.
